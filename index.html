Welcome to word_net.

I hope you appreciate the similarity to Skynet (as in the evil robot overlords from the Terminator movies). Word_net is the friendly (we hope!) version that takes enormous piles of textual data and finds meaning for us.

Tools

We’ve used NLTK and Scikit-Learn. The project also relies heavily on wordnet - the sematic web
